Momo


A classic Nepali dumpling—momos are delicate pockets of seasoned filling wrapped in thin dough and traditionally steamed (or fried).
Widely loved across Nepal, they showcase simple ingredients transformed into a comforting, hand‑held dish served with a spicy tomato‑sesame chutney.

Introduction

  • Prepare filling
    Soft, slightly chewy wrappers encase a savory filling that can be meat, vegetables, or a mix.
    Key characteristics: a light, neutral dough, tightly sealed pleats, and a juicy interior.
    Served hot with a tangy, chili‑garlic dipping sauce and optional achar (pickle).

    Ingredients

    Dough

    • 3 cups all‑purpose flour
    • 1 tsp salt
    • ~1 cup warm water (adjust as needed)
    • 1 tbsp oil (optional)

    Chicken Momo filling

    • 500 g ground chicken (or pork/beef/tofu/shredded veggies)
    • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
    • 2–3 green onions, finely sliced
    • 2–3 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
    • 1–2 tbsp soy sauce
    • 1 tsp sesame oil
    • ½ tsp ground black pepper
    • 1 tsp salt (adjust)
    • ¼ cup finely shredded cabbage or carrot (optional, for moisture)

    Instructions

    1. Make the dough
      mix flour and salt,add warm water gradually,knead until smooth and elastic(~8-10min).
      Cover and rest 30 min.
    2. Prepare filling
      combine ground meat/veggies with onion,green onion,garlic,ginger,soy sauce,sesame oil,pepper, salt and shredded veg.
      Mix until well combined; chilling 15-30 min improves texture.
    3. Roll wrappers
      divide dough into golf-ball pieces.Roll each into a thin circle(~7-8cm).
      Keep covered to prevent drying
    4. Fill and shape
      place ~1 tbsp filling in center of each wrapper.
      Fold and pleat edges to seal (traditional pleat,half-moon,or simple fold).Ensure no holes.
    5. Steam
      arrange momos in a greased steamer basket,leaving space.
      Steam over boiling water for 10-12 minutes(meat momos) or 8-10 for veg.
    6. Make chutney
      blend roasted tomatoes,chilies,garlic,sesame/peanuts,salt and lemon/vinegar until smooth.Adjust spice and salt.
    7. Serve
      plate momos hot with chutney and a drizzle of sesame oil or a side of achar.For fried momos,pan-fry steamed momos lightly in oil until crisp.
